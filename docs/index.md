# About Me

I am a Chief Data Engineer at [Deutsche Post DHL Group](https://www.dpdhl.jobs/data-science) and an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at [Universum College](https://www.universum-ks.org/).

I obtained my PhD from [University of Bonn](https://www.uni-bonn.de/) in the [Smart Data Analytics (SDA)](http://sda.tech) group under the supervision of [Prof. Dr. Jens Lehmann](http://jens-lehmann.org/). Before that, I completed my MSc at the [University of Prishtina](http://uni-pr.edu/), supervised by [Prof. Dr. Lule Ahmedi](http://luleahmedi.uni-pr.edu/).

My research and engineering interests include Semantic Web, Big Data, Machine Learning, and distributed systems such as [Apache Spark](http://spark.apache.org/) and [Apache Flink](http://flink.apache.org/).

## Interests

- Big Data
- Data Mining and Data Analysis
- Semantic Web and Semantic Search
- Machine Learning
- Distributed Computing
